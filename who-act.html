Key Health Acts and Policies

International Health Regulations

The International Health Regulations (2005) are a legally binding instrument of international law that aims to prevent and respond to the international spread of diseases.

Framework Convention on Tobacco Control (FCTC)

The WHO FCTC is the first international treaty negotiated under the auspices of WHO to protect present and future generations from the consequences of tobacco consumption.

Global Strategy on Human Resources for Health

This act focuses on strengthening the global health workforce and ensuring universal health coverage through equitable distribution of health workers.

WHO Pandemic Agreement

The WHO is working on a global pandemic agreement to strengthen preparedness and response to future pandemics.

Essential Medicines and Health Products

This initiative aims to improve access to safe, effective, and affordable medicines and vaccines worldwide.
